Angelo's Ice Cream

Here at Angelo's Ice Cream Parlor, we've been serving up scoops of fun since 1955!

Located in the heart of downtown, we are Pensacola's premier ice cream destination. With over 30 different flavors and our renowned toppings bar, the possibilities are nearly endless! Cones, cups, sundaes, milkshakes, soft serve - we've got something for everyone.

We are open everyday from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, so stop on by!
